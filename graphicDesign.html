My Graphic Design Projects

Discover the wonderous graphic design projects of Eric Ji.

Chinese Student Association 2022-2023

When the first design competition arose at my high school for my school's Chinese Student Association, I wanted to make sure that my designs made an impression. When I thought of Chinese culture, I remembered the unique design of watercolor and how beautiful it looked. Along with water color, ink has always been a defining characteristic of Chinese Culture. It is the combination of both that I knew would help me win the competition

Korean Culture Club 2022-2023

Having more experiance with my previous two projects, I wanted my last and final hoodie design to be truly iconic. Initially, I was unfamiliar with Korean culture, the art, the food, and the people. However, as I had many Korean friends, I was able to gain more insight. One of the most recognizable aspects of Korean culture is their food, and Buldak is one of the most iconic ramen mascots. With this in mind, I decided to create a design that would be both simple, recognizable, and most importantly: funny.

Vietnamese Culture Club 2022-2023

While this design was originally intended for CSA, I recalled the previous year's cultural performance by the Vietnamese Culture Club. The club had created a beautiful river boat sequence where among the audience was brought to the Vietnamese rivers. It is because of this that I was inspired to breathe new life into the design and create a new version for the Vietnamese Culture Club.

Chinese Student Association Alternative

As if two designs for Chinese Student Association wasn't enough, I went ahead and created another one. Inspired by the paintings of birds and the rising sun, I created a simple maroon design that would be both simple and elegant. With the words of a beautiful poem on the side, it is both an aesthetic and simple narrative.
